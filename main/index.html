Barbaros' Summer School

An English Story Game

Are you ready to step into a new country, a new culture, and a completely new adventure?
In this interactive story game, you play as Barbaros, a high school student traveling to Italy for summer school. What begins as a simple trip soon turns into a journey filled with unexpected conversations, cultural surprises, challenges, and important choices.
From the airport departure to the streets of Rome, every chapter brings new characters, mini-games, and dialogue-based challenges designed to improve your English while keeping you inside the story.
Your choices matter.
Your answers shape the journey.
Your English skills will grow with every interaction.
Will you handle the pressure of speaking a new language abroad?
Will you make the right decisions when it counts?
Start your journey and discover what awaits you beyond the departure gate.
